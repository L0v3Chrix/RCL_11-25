### Annotations

**Header (Sticky):**
- Logo: 140px wide, links to homepage
- Navigation: Collapsible hamburger on mobile
- SMS icon: Quick access to text help (always visible)
- Background: White with subtle shadow on scroll

**Hero Section:**
- Height: 100vh (desktop), 80vh (mobile)
- Background: Authentic photo of RCL house (warm, inviting)
- Overlay: Dark gradient (bottom to top, 60% opacity)
- Text: White with subtle text shadow for readability
- CTAs: Sunset orange (Apply) + White outline (Tour)

**Founder Story:**
- Layout: Image left, text right (desktop) / Stacked (mobile)
- Photo: Circular, 200px diameter (Slade & Chloe together)
- Quote: Large, Caveat font for warmth
- Video CTA: Play button with preview thumbnail

**House Showcase:**
- Grid: 3 columns desktop, 1 column mobile (swipeable)
- Cards: Rounded corners, warm shadow, hover lift
- Availability badge: Green (available) / Orange (waitlist)
- Photos: 16:10 aspect ratio, high quality

**Success Stories:**
- Background: Light cream (stands out from white)
- Border: Left accent bar (sunset orange)
- Quote: Large, Caveat font
- Video thumbnail: Click to play modal

**Process (What to Expect):**
- Icons: Large, clear (numbered 1-4)
- Layout: 4 columns desktop / 2x2 grid mobile
- CTA: Center aligned, prominent

**Crisis Support:**
- Background: Soft warning yellow
- Icons: Large, clear contact methods
- Text: High contrast for urgency
- CTA: Link to comprehensive crisis resources

**Footer:**
- Background: Warm dark brown
- Text: Light cream
- Links: Organized in columns (desktop) / Stacked (mobile)
- Login links: Prominent for returning users

## Responsive Breakpoints

**Desktop (1280px+):**
- Multi-column layouts (2-3 columns)
- Larger images and typography
- Hover states active
- More content visible above fold

**Tablet (768px - 1279px):**
- 2-column layouts or single column
- Medium-sized images
- Touch-optimized (larger tap targets)
- Collapsible sections

**Mobile (< 768px):**
- Single column layouts
- Swipeable carousels for houses
- Hamburger navigation
- Sticky CTAs
- Progressive disclosure (accordion sections)
