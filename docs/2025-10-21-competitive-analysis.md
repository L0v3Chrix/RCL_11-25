# Competitive Analysis - Sober Living Websites

**Date:** October 21, 2025
**Purpose:** Analyze competitor websites to identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for RCL redesign
**Methodology:** Direct website analysis of 5 Austin-area competitors + current RCL site

---

## Executive Summary

**Key Findings:**
1. **Industry standard is basic** - Most sober living sites use templated WordPress designs with minimal customization
2. **Huge opportunity for differentiation** - None of the competitors have exceptional design or UX
3. **Trust-building is inconsistent** - Some sites use testimonials well, others lack social proof entirely
4. **Mobile experience is poor** - Most sites have responsive layouts but poor mobile optimization
5. **Conversion paths are unclear** - CTAs are scattered, forms are generic, no progressive intake systems observed
6. **Content is text-heavy** - Long paragraphs, minimal visual storytelling, few videos
7. **RCL current site needs complete overhaul** - Outdated design, poor UX, lacks professional polish

**Competitive Advantage Opportunities for RCL:**
- **Exceptional design** - None of the competitors have award-worthy design (we can be first)
- **Digital intake system** - No one offers 3-step progressive forms (we'll be innovative)
- **Video storytelling** - Competitors use minimal video (we can dominate with Slade/Chloe's story)
- **Real-time availability** - No competitor shows house availability in real-time
- **Family engagement** - No competitor offers family portals (massive differentiator)
- **Authentic photography** - All competitors use mix of stock and low-quality photos (we can stand out)
